Physical description
Heavyset and deliberate in his movements, with close-cropped silver hair and a jaw like a barge prow. Always immaculate — black court dress, obsidian ring on the left hand. His eyes are the grey of old ice, seldom blinking.
Personality & voice
Speaks in full, measured sentences, never raises his voice. Believes patience is the only weapon worth carrying. Has never raised a hand directly — he sends others. The most dangerous person in any room precisely because he appears to be the calmest.
Backstory — matches your search
Commander of the Obsidian Court. Varek's reach extends across all three northern provinces. Seized power during the Succession Wars by being the last man standing who nobody suspected. Kael Draveth's tribunal was engineered by Varek personally — he saw Kael as a future threat and moved first.
