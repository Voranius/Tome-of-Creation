Overview

The Ashwood is one of the oldest locations in the Veldrath continent. The elder druids of the Greenmantle Circle claim it predates their order by millennia.

Key facts:

— Does not age like normal forests
— Trees appear almost iridescent in low light
— Arcane workings behave unpredictably inside its borders
— Connected to The Sundering War through the Binding of Caldrath

Aria's connection

Aria learned about the Ashwood at Greyveil Academy — surface history only. The deeper truth (Caldrath is not fully dormant) she discovers through her own research in Chapter 4.
